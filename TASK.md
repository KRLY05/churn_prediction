# Telco Customer Churn Prediction Task

## Project Context
You are working for a telco company providing home phone and Internet services to customers. Recently, many customers have left the company. This phenomenon, known as **churn**, is a major problem that needs to be tackled.

In the telco industry, it is well known that the cost of retaining an existing customer is far less than acquiring a new one. Hence, being able to predict churn is critical for business stakeholders.

## Objective
The **AI Exploitation and Innovation** team has been tasked to:
- Predict churn for the next quarter (**Q3 2021**).
- Identify which customers are most likely to churn.
- Enable proactive retention strategies (e.g., offering better deals).

## Dataset
- **Timeframe**: Customers in Q1-Q2 2021.
- **Files**: 
  - `Telco-Customer-Churn.csv`: Relevant customer data.
  - `data_description.txt`: Metadata and column descriptions.

## General Guidelines
- **Methodology**: Explain your process and findings clearly.
- **Assumptions**: State any assumptions made during analysis.
- **Readability**: Ensure code is clean and understandable by team members.
- **Format**: Use a Jupyter Notebook to walk through your analysis and train of thought.

---

## Part 1: Finding Customers at Risk (Predicting Churn)

### Context
Create a model to identify customers likely to churn in the next quarter for campaign managers to include in marketing selections.

### Requirements
- Use **Python** or **R**.
- Focus on how the model will be used in practice.
- Define and track relevant **KPIs and metrics**.
